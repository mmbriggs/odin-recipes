Creamy Pasta

This dish can take many different forms depending on what ingredients you currently have in your pantry. The one non-negotiable element is that it MUST be creamy. If she had her way, my child would eat creamy pasta every single day for the rest of her life and honestly, I relate to that.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (typically penne, but whatever you can find will work)
  • Pesto
  • Heavy Cream
  • A Metric Shit Ton of Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • Chicken Breast, diced

Method

  1. Fill a pot of water, add salt, and bring it to a boil.
  2. While you wait for the water to boil, grill diced chicken in a separate pan at medium-high heat. Turn heat to low when the chicken is almost cooked through.
  3. Add pasta to boiling water.
  4. Pour a half cup (I think? I eyeball it tbh) of heavy cream into the pan with your chicken. Stir until it begins to thicken.
  5. Add a cup of grated parmesan and stir until creamy. Again- the creamy part is crucial.
  6. Drain the pasta and add the chicken and creamy sauce mixture to it. Stir until combined.
  7. Sigh in resignation when your child ineveitably declares that it needs more salt.
