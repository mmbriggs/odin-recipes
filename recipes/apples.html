Apples

These are apples. You can buy them at most stores. Legends say that eating one a day will keep the doctors away. It is the one food my child will eat without fail.

Ingredients

  • Soil
  • Water
  • Sunlight
  • Apple seeds
  • Patience

Method

  1. Get in the car.
  2. Drive to the store (preferably HEB).
  3. Find the produce section.
  4. Grab a big ole bag of Red Delicious.
  5. Pay.
  6. Breathe a sigh of relief knowing your child will no longer be surviving solely on air and juice pouches.
