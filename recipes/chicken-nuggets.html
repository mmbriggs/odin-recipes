Chicken Nuggets

Chicken nuggets have magical properties. They are the sole source of protein for picky children all over the world. Parents both loathe and worship at the altar of chicken nuggets.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Probably a lot of other things I don't want to think about

Method 1

  1. Remove bag of frozen nuggets from freezer and read the cooking instructions for the 100th time.
  2. Preheat oven to specified degrees.
  3. Plop the frozen nuggets onto a baking sheet.
  4. Read the cooking instructions for the 101th time. Cook the nuggets for the specified minutes.
  5. Serve with ketchup and an apple, so you can say that you also gave your child a fruit and vegetable.
  6. Spend all the time you saved not cooking and not dirtying dishes scrolling TikTok on the couch.
